                                                                ***** VARIABLE NAMING CONVENTION AND RULES *****                                   
            
                                    
                    1.)           Can be- Letters (Upper or lower case)                               |        Example- name= "Vipul"
                                          Digits                                                      |                 Name= "Akshat"
                                          Underscore                                                  |               Both are different
                    2.)           Must start with-  underscore or Letter                              |
                    3.)           Can't start with number                                             |        Also, if name="sun"
                    4.)           Case sensitive                                                      |         and later you changed it to name= "Moon"
                    5.)           Can't use python keywords(if, else , while , for , def, class etc.) |             It will give--->  moon
                                                                                                      |       Will give the last change done as the output
