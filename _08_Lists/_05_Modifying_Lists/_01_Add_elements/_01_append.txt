1.) Adding an Element (append)

li = [1, 2, 3]
li.append(4)
print(li)

Output

[1, 2, 3, 4]

2.)
fruits = ["apple", "banana", "mango"] 
fruits2 = ("mango" , "kiwi")
fruits.append(fruits2)    # here in 'append' you are inserting fruits2 as a whole new list ['apple', 'banana', 'mango', ('mango', 'kiwi')]
print(fruits)

['apple', 'banana', 'mango', ('mango', 'kiwi')]
