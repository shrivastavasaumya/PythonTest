5.) Nested Tuple (Tuple inside Tuple)

~ A tuple inside another tuple

t = (1, 2, (3, 4), 5)
 Accessing Nested Elements:
print(t[2])      # (3, 4)
print(t[2][0])   # 3
print(t[2][1])   # 4

=> Structure:

Index:   0   1    2       3
Value:   1   2  (3,4)     5
                ↑
            inner tuple
