                                                        Polymorphism
                                                            
                                                poly is many + morph is forms 
It refers to methods/functions/operators with the same name that can be executed on many objects or classes.

                                
Types of Polymorphism :-

A) Function over-riding 
B) Duck Typing
C) Function overloading (not possible in Python)
D) Function overloading  v/s Operator overloading
